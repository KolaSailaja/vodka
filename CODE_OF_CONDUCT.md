# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Vodka project and community a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

Regardless of:
- experience level
- gender
- identity
- nationality
- race
- religion
- personal background

everyone should feel welcome to contribute.

---

# Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Helping others learn
- Focusing on collaboration
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional environment

---

# Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying acceptable behavior
- Reviewing contributions fairly
- Taking appropriate action when necessary
- Maintaining a healthy and productive community

---

# Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may result in:
- warnings
- temporary bans
- permanent bans

depending on severity.

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within:
- GitHub issues
- pull requests
- discussions
- project communication spaces

---

# Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private issue or contact the maintainers directly.

All reports will be reviewed fairly and handled appropriately.

---

# Final Note

Be respectful.

We're all here because we enjoy building cool things with Go.
